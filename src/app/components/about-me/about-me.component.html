My Creative Process
How I do it?
How Might We..?
When I begin a project, I use a process called Design Thinking. It involves understanding people's needs and using creative thinking to solve their problems. The goal is to come up with a prototype (a simplified version of the final product) that helps us figure out what needs to be improved, make sure the way users will interact with it makes sense, and guide our future work on the project.
